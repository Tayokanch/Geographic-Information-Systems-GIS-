## How to Render/Load Lat and Long data from a CSV file in QGIS

- Navigate to the Top Menu, click on `Layer` and select `Add Layer`
- Click on `Add vector Data`
- Load in the CSV `Vector Data` we want to use 
- fill in the `X_POSSIBLE_NAME` = `longitude` (This is the longitude field on the CVS file)
- fill in the `Y_POSSIBLE_NAME` = `latitude` (This is the latitude field on the CVS file)
- This will Automatically load our file to the layer
